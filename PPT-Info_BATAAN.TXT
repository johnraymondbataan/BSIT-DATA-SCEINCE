TYPES OF DATA 

STRUCTURED DATA STRUCTURED DATA IS QUANTITATIVE DATA IN THE FORM OF NUMBERS AND, VALUES 
UNSTRUCTURED DATA UNSTRUCTURED DATA IS QUALITATIVE DATA IN FORM OF TEXT FILES, AUDIO FILES, AND VIDEO FILES


DATA 

refers to a collection of facts, statistics, or information that is gathered through various sources or observations. It can be in
the form of numbers, text, images, audio, or any other format that can be processed by a computer. Data can be raw and
unprocessed, or it can be structured and organized 


DATA SET 

is a specific collection of data points or observations. It is a structured representation of data organized into rows
(also called records) and columns (also called attributes or variables).


TYPES OF DATA SET 

- RECORD
- GRAPH
- ORDERED
- MULTIMEDIA

STANDARD DATA SCIENCE TASKS 

Data science tasks typically involve a variety of activities aimed at extracting insights or knowledge from data.
Some of the standard data science tasks include: 

- Data Collection 
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing 
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) 
- Feature Engineering 
- Model Selection and Training 
- Model Evaluation and Validation 


ROLE OF DATA SCIENCE IN GAME DEVELOPMENT 

Gaming companies have become an integral part of the global entertainment industry, with users spending more
time than ever before in gaming. The use of data in gaming is to create strategies based on the player’s behavior
and to constantly collect data in order to make predictions and decisions. 



GAME DEVELOPMENT

Game Development is the art of creating games and describes the design, development and release of a game.
It may involve concept generation, design, build, test and release. While you create a game, it is important
to think about the game mechanics, rewards, player engagement and level design.
